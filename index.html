About Sordish

Sordish is characterized by flowing consonants and melodic vowel combinations. The language emphasizes harmony with nature and spiritual connection.

History

The Sordish language traces its origins back to the East Merkopan tribes, evolving over centuries into a refined cultural identity.

Grammar

Sordish follows a grammar close to that of English and German, and is a fusional language. It has three grammatical genders.
